# Code of Conduct

This project adopts the [Contributor Covenant](https://www.contributor-covenant.org/),
version 2.1.

In short: be respectful and constructive. Harassment, discrimination, and
personal attacks are not tolerated. Assume good faith, keep discussion focused
on the work, and help make participation a positive experience for everyone.

## Reporting

Report unacceptable behaviour to **<dhira@wigataintech.com>**. Reports will be handled confidentially.

The full text of the Contributor Covenant 2.1 is available at
https://www.contributor-covenant.org/version/2/1/code_of_conduct/.
